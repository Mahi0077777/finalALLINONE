Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upload my own fonts for custom watermarks?

Yes, you can upload custom font files (TTF, OTF) to create truly unique text watermarks that match your brand or personal style.

How complex can custom watermarks be?

Custom watermarks can be very complex, including multiple text elements, images, shapes, and effects combined in layered designs.

Can I save my custom watermark designs for future use?

Yes, you can save your custom designs as templates and reuse them across multiple documents or share them with your team.

Are there any limitations on custom watermark colors?

No, you have full color control with support for HEX, RGB, and CMYK color values, allowing exact color matching to your brand.

Can custom watermarks include interactive elements?

While basic interactive elements are supported, most custom watermarks are static. Advanced interactive features may require specialized PDF software.
