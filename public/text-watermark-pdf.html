Frequently Asked Questions

What font should I use for text watermarks?

Choose clean, readable fonts like Arial, Helvetica, or Times New Roman. Consider your document's existing fonts for consistency.

How transparent should text watermarks be?

Typically 20-40% opacity works well - visible enough to read but subtle enough not to interfere with document content.

Can I use different text on different pages?

Yes, you can apply different text watermarks to specific pages or page ranges within the same document.

What's the best position for text watermarks?

Center or diagonal placement works well for visibility, while corner placement is more subtle. Choose based on your needs.

Can I include special characters in text watermarks?

Yes, you can include copyright symbols (©), registered trademarks (®), and other special characters in your text watermarks.
