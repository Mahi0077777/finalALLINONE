How to Combine PDF Files on Any Device - No App Required

Need to merge PDFs but using different devices? Our online PDF combiner works flawlessly across all platforms - whether you're on an iPhone, Android, Windows PC, or Mac. No app installations needed, just open your browser and start combining documents in seconds.

Device-Specific Instructions

On iPhone or iPad

  1. Open Safari or Chrome on your iOS device
  2. Go to freepropdf.com/merge-pdf
  3. Tap "Select Files" and choose from:
    • iCloud Drive
    • Files app
    • Photos
    • Or take a photo with your camera
  4. Arrange pages if needed
  5. Tap "Merge" and save to your device

On Android Phones & Tablets

  1. Open Chrome or your preferred browser
  2. Visit our PDF merger page
  3. Select files from:
    • Google Drive
    • Downloads folder
    • Or take a photo
  4. Drag to reorder pages (if needed)
  5. Download the merged file to your device

On Windows PC

  1. Open any modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox)
  2. Go to our PDF combiner tool
  3. Drag and drop files or click to browse
  4. Use the preview to arrange pages
  5. Click "Merge" and save the combined PDF

On Mac Computers

  1. Open Safari, Chrome, or Firefox
  2. Visit freepropdf.com/merge-pdf
  3. Select files from your Mac or cloud storage
  4. Use the intuitive interface to organize pages
  5. Download or save directly to iCloud

Cloud Storage Integration

Access your files from anywhere by connecting to popular cloud services:

  • OneDrive - Perfect for Windows and Office 365 users
  • iCloud - Ideal for Apple ecosystem users

No need to download files first - our tool works directly with your cloud storage.

Common Device-Specific Issues & Solutions

Files Not Uploading on Mobile?

Try this: Clear your browser cache or try a different browser. Ensure you have a stable internet connection.

Slow Performance on Older Devices?

Solution: Try our PDF compressor first to reduce file sizes before merging.

Can't Find Merged File?

Check: Your device's Downloads folder or the "Files" app. Use the search function with the filename.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to install any software?

No, our PDF combiner works directly in your web browser on any device without requiring additional software.

Is there a file size limit for mobile devices?

The maximum file size is 50MB total across all files, regardless of device. For larger files, try our compression tool first.

Can I merge PDFs offline?

An internet connection is required to use our online tool. For offline use, you'll need to download a desktop application.

How do I share the merged PDF from my phone?

After merging, tap the share icon and choose your preferred sharing method: email, messages, or save to cloud storage.
