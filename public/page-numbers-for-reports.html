Numbering Conventions

Business report numbering rules:

  • Executive Summary: Roman numerals (i, ii, iii...)
  • Table of Contents: Continue Roman numerals
  • Main Body: Arabic numerals starting from 1
  • Appendices: Continue Arabic numerals
  • References: Continue Arabic numerals

Report Structure and Numbering

Front Matter Numbering

Preliminary section formatting:

  • Cover Page: No page number
  • Title Page: No page number
  • Executive Summary: Roman numeral "i"
  • Table of Contents: Continue Roman numerals
  • List of Figures/Tables: Continue Roman numerals
  • Introduction: Roman numerals or start Arabic

Main Body Numbering

Primary content section:

  • Methodology: Starts with Arabic "1"
  • Findings/Results: Continue sequentially
  • Analysis: Continue sequentially
  • Discussion: Continue sequentially
  • Conclusions: Continue sequentially

Back Matter Numbering

Supporting documentation:

  • Recommendations: Continue Arabic numerals
  • Appendices: Continue Arabic numerals
  • References: Continue Arabic numerals
  • Glossary: Continue Arabic numerals
  • Index: Continue Arabic numerals

Frequently Asked Questions

Should my report cover page have a page number?

Typically, cover pages do not have page numbers. Numbering usually starts with the executive summary or table of contents using Roman numerals.

What's the best position for page numbers in business reports?

Bottom center is the most common and professional position for business reports, though bottom right is also widely used.

Can I include my company logo with page numbers?

Yes, you can incorporate company logos or other branding elements with your page numbers for a more professional corporate appearance.

How do I handle numbering for reports with multiple sections?

Use Roman numerals for front matter (executive summary, table of contents) and Arabic numerals starting from 1 for the main body, continuing through all sections.

Should confidential reports have special numbering?

Yes, confidential reports often include "Confidential" markings or document control numbers along with page numbers for security purposes.
