Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best angle for diagonal watermarks?

45 degrees is the standard and most effective angle for diagonal watermarks, providing good visibility while maintaining document readability.

Will diagonal watermarks interfere with document content?

When properly configured with appropriate transparency (20-40%), diagonal watermarks provide protection without significantly interfering with content readability.

Can I use different diagonal angles on different pages?

Yes, you can apply different diagonal angles to specific pages or page ranges within the same document for varied security levels.

How do diagonal watermarks affect PDF printing?

Diagonal watermarks print well when configured with appropriate opacity and color. Always test print samples to ensure good results.

Are diagonal watermarks more secure than other placements?

Diagonal watermarks are generally more secure because they span across content areas, making them harder to remove without affecting document content.
