    Frequently Asked Questions

    Are sample watermarks the same as final watermarks?

    Sample watermarks use "SAMPLE" text to indicate they're for testing purposes, but otherwise have the same functionality and appearance as final watermarks.

    Can I save my sample watermark designs?

    Yes, you can save sample configurations and apply them to actual documents once you're satisfied with the design.

    What documents should I use for watermark samples?

    Use documents similar to your actual use case - business documents for corporate use, academic papers for educational use, etc.

    How many sample variations should I test?

    Test 3-5 different variations to get a good range of options without overwhelming decision-making.

    Can sample watermarks be applied to multiple pages?

    Yes, sample watermarks can be applied to single pages, specific page ranges, or all pages in the document for comprehensive testing.
