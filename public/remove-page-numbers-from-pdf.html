Types of Page Number Elements to Remove

Standard Page Numbers

Common numbering formats:

  • Arabic Numbers: 1, 2, 3, 4...
  • Roman Numerals: i, ii, iii, iv...
  • Alphabetic: A, B, C, D...
  • With Prefixes: Page 1, Pg. 2, P3...
  • With Suffixes: 1 of 10, 2/50...

Headers and Footers

Additional elements to remove:

  • Document Titles: Headers with document names
  • Chapter Names: Section titles in headers
  • Author Information: Author names in footers
  • Date Stamps: Dates in headers/footers
  • Company Information: Business details in footers

Complex Elements

Advanced removal scenarios:

  • Bates Numbers: Legal document identifiers
  • Watermarks: Background text or images
  • Stamps: "Confidential", "Draft" markings
  • Line Separators: Lines above/below numbers
  • Decorative Elements: Graphics with numbers
