Frequently Asked Questions

Can all watermarks be removed from PDFs?

Most watermarks can be removed, but success depends on the watermark type, placement, and complexity. Some embedded watermarks may be more challenging to remove.

Will removing watermarks affect my document quality?

Our tool is designed to preserve document quality while removing watermarks. However, some minor quality changes may occur depending on the watermark complexity.

Is it legal to remove watermarks from PDFs?

Only remove watermarks from documents you own or have permission to modify. Removing watermarks from copyrighted content without permission may violate copyright laws.

How long does watermark removal take?

Processing time varies based on document size, complexity, and the number of watermarks. Most documents are processed within a few minutes.

Can I remove watermarks from password-protected PDFs?

You'll need to remove password protection first using our PDF protection tool, then you can remove watermarks normally.
