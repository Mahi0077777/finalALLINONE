Frequently Asked Questions

What transparency level should I use for watermarks?

For most documents, 30-50% opacity provides good balance between visibility and subtlety. Use higher transparency (70-90%) for very subtle marking.

Will transparent watermarks affect PDF printing quality?

Transparent watermarks generally print well, but test your specific opacity level and printer to ensure good results.

Can transparent watermarks be removed from PDFs?

Removing watermarks from PDFs is difficult regardless of transparency. Always keep an unwatermarked original for future use.

What colors work best for transparent watermarks?

Light gray, faded brand colors, or white text on dark backgrounds work well. Avoid colors that blend too much with content.

How do transparent watermarks affect file size?

Transparent watermarks have minimal impact on file size, especially compared to solid watermarks, making them efficient for large documents.
