Step-by-Step for Each Device

On iPhone or iPad

  1. Open Safari or Chrome on your iOS device
  2. Visit freepropdf.com/merge-pdf and bookmark for easy access
  3. Tap "Select Files" and choose from:
    • iCloud Drive
    • Files app
    • Photos
    • Take a photo with your camera
  4. Arrange pages by dragging them into order
  5. Tap "Merge" and save to Files or share directly

Pro Tip: Use the Files app to organize your merged documents in folders.

On Android Phones & Tablets

  1. Open Chrome or your preferred browser
  2. Go to our PDF merger and add to home screen for app-like access
  3. Select files from:
    • Google Drive
    • Downloads folder
    • Gallery/Photos
    • Camera to capture new documents
  4. Reorder pages with touch gestures
  5. Merge and download to your device or cloud storage

Pro Tip: Use Android's share menu to quickly send merged files to other apps.

On Windows Tablets

  1. Open Edge or Chrome in tablet mode
  2. Navigate to our merger with touch or stylus
  3. Upload files from OneDrive or local storage
  4. Use touch controls to arrange pages
  5. Save merged file to your preferred location

Pro Tip: Windows tablets work great with stylus input for precise page arrangement.

Common Mobile Issues & Solutions

Files Not Uploading?

Solutions:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Clear browser cache and data
  • Try a different browser
  • Restart your device
  • Ensure files aren't corrupted

App Crashing or Freezing?

Try these fixes:

  • Close other apps running in background
  • Free up device storage space
  • Update your browser to latest version
  • Reduce file sizes before merging
  • Restart your device

Can't Find Downloaded File?

Check these locations:

  • Downloads folder in Files app
  • Browser download history
  • Search by filename
  • Check cloud storage if auto-save enabled

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this work on iPhone and Android?

Yes, our PDF merger works perfectly on all mobile devices including iPhone, iPad, Android phones and tablets, and Windows tablets.

Can I access cloud storage from mobile?

Yes, you can directly access Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, and iCloud from your mobile device when using our merger.

What's the file size limit on phones?

The maximum file size is 50MB total across all files, regardless of device. For larger files, try our compression tool first.

How do I share merged PDFs from my phone?

After merging, use your device's share menu to send via email, messaging apps, or save to cloud storage. All standard sharing options are available.

Do I need to install an app?

No app installation required! Our tool works directly in your mobile browser. You can add it to your home screen for quick access.
