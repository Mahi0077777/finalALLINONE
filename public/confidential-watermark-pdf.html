Frequently Asked Questions

Are confidential watermarks legally binding?

While not legally binding themselves, confidential watermarks serve as notice of confidentiality and can support legal claims in case of unauthorized disclosure.

What color should confidential watermarks be?

Red is traditionally used for confidential watermarks as it's highly visible and universally recognized as a warning color, but dark gray or blue can also work.

Should confidential watermarks be on every page?

Yes, confidential watermarks should appear on every page to maintain security throughout the document and prevent page separation.

Can I add recipient names to confidential watermarks?

Yes, adding recipient names or user IDs to watermarks enhances security by making documents traceable to specific individuals.

How transparent should confidential watermarks be?

Confidential watermarks should be visible enough to be easily read but transparent enough not to completely obscure document content.
