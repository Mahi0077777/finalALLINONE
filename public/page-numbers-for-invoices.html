Frequently Asked Questions

Are page numbers required on invoices?

While not always legally required, page numbers are highly recommended for multi-page invoices to maintain professionalism and prevent confusion.

What's the best position for page numbers on invoices?

Bottom center is the most professional and commonly used position for invoice page numbers, though bottom right is also acceptable.

Should I include "Page X of Y" on invoices?

Yes, including total page count (e.g., "Page 1 of 3") is helpful for clients to verify they have the complete invoice.

Can I include my invoice number with page numbers?

Absolutely. Including invoice numbers with page numbers (e.g., "INV-1234 - Page 1") helps with document tracking and organization.

How do page numbers affect invoice processing?

Page numbers help accounting departments process invoices more efficiently and reduce errors in handling multi-page documents.
